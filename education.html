Education

Educational Enhancement

In order to maximize employability, a rising psychologist would ideally earn a Master's Degree or Ph.D to start. Psychologists can also undergo multiple types of training, such as becoming board certified as a specialist in the field or becoming a licensed psychologist. In order to be board certified, applicants can select from any 15 subcategories offered, and by becoming labeled as a specialist in that category, their employability status will rise greatly. This is also true for licensed psychologists, but in order to earn a license, most applicants are required to have earned a Ph.D. The final and most common way to raise employability as a psychologist is through interning in the field is highly recognized and appreciated by those scouting psychologists for jobs, since they value the real-life experience very highly.
