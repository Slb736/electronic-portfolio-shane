Career Summary

Career: Psychologist
Description: Psychology is a career that focuses on the workings of the human mind, and has many subcategories within the career, such as biopsychology, cognitive psychology, and social psychology. Psychology is a field that generally focuses on the processes behind the behavior of people, such as their emotions and social situation, and use these studies to attempt to better people's situation.
Education: Required education for the field varies based on the outlet, meaning that careers that deal with patients will require more education, typically a Ph.D, while careers that do not focus on dealing with patients can take either a Bachelor's or a Master's Degree.
Skills: Skills that are commonly required in the field include patience, communication, dedication, observational skills, problem-solving and emotional stability.
Job Outlook: The starting salary for a psychologist can vary heavily, and across the scope of the career, can span anywhere from $50k to $100k. In order to earn a higher position in the career, the average time requirement is about 9 years.
